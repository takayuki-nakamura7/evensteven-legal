EvenSteven - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

EvenSteven (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a household management app for couples. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google, we also receive your profile picture URL from Google.

Household data: Information you provide about your household, including household name, and currency preference.

App usage data: Chore logs (what was completed and when), expense records (amounts, descriptions, and who paid), settlement records, weekly check-in responses, and notification preferences. This data is created by you within the app.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, location, or photo library
  • We do not process payments or store financial account information

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app’s features: tracking chores, splitting expenses, and showing your household’s fairness dashboard. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase: We use Supabase to store your data and handle authentication. Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database secured with row-level security policies, ensuring you can only access data belonging to your household.

Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, authentication is handled through Google’s OAuth service. We receive only your email, name, and profile picture URL.

5. Notifications

The app schedules local notifications on your device (e.g., weekly check-in reminders). These are processed entirely on your device and no notification data is sent to external servers.

6. Data Sharing

Your household data (chores, expenses, check-ins) is shared only with your household partner within the app. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete specific data at any time from within the app (chore logs, expenses, or all chores). If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers.

8. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption. Access to your household data is enforced at the database level through row-level security policies, ensuring only members of your household can access your data.

9. Children’s Privacy

EvenSteven is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at support@evensteven.xyz.
